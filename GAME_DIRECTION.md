# Game Direction - Alien Ship Synthetics

## Core Concept
Players control synthetics sent to a derelict alien ship to eliminate organic threats and collect valuable artifacts. The mission is chaotic, dangerous, and requires strategic decision-making.

## Game Type
- **Inspiration:** Lethal Company (but different)
- **Game Loop:** Round-based (not match-based)
- **Progression:** Permanent upgrades between rounds

## Technical Specs

### Camera & View
- **First-Person perspective**
- Immersive, visceral gameplay

### Enemy Design
- **Variety:** Lots of different monster types
- **Behavior:** Each creature type has unique AI behaviors
  - Different attack patterns
  - Different hunting styles
  - Different threat levels
- **Spawning:** Scaled difficulty as rounds progress

### Artifact Collection
- **Mechanic:** Simple pickup system
- **Purpose:** Collect artifacts from the ship
- **Risk/Reward:** Artifacts may be in dangerous locations

### Progression System
- **Between Rounds:** Permanent upgrades unlock
- **Difficulty Scaling:** Each round gets harder
- **Meta-Progression:** Players unlock new abilities/tools as they progress

### Atmosphere
- Dark, tense, dangerous environment
- Alien ship aesthetic
- Organic threats vs. synthetic player character
